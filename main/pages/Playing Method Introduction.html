    Playing Method Introduction

    Each character in the game can carry a skill. The skills carried by different professions have different effects. For example, the silver gray shown is a guard. The skills he carries can improve his survivability. The map is divided into ground and high platform, because professional characteristics cannot be deployed in positions allowed by non-professionals, for example, melee characters cannot be deployed on high platforms.

    Arknight contains a variety of enemies that need to be eliminated by characters with corresponding occupations. For example, we need characters whose occupation is sniper to eliminate some flying units. Of course, other occupations can also do this, and it may take more time to eliminate.
