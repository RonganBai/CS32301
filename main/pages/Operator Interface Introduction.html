    Operator Interface Introduction

    On the left is the attribute panel, and below it is the rarity of the character. The higher the rarity, the higher the basic attributes of the character. Below is the character's name, class, attack range, and character traits.

    On the right is the character's level and skills. Characters with a rarity greater than 3 stars can be upgraded twice. 6-star characters have 3 skills, 5-4 star characters have 2 skills, 3-star characters have 1 skill, and 2-1 star characters have no skills. The lower right corner is the characteristic talent of the character
