    • Arkningts Introduction

      Arknights is a tactical mobile tower defense game developed by Hypergryph and Studio Montagne, and published by Yostar. It was first released in China in May 2019 and later made available internationally in January 2020.

      Tower Defense Game Introduction

      Desktop Tower Defense: Created by Paul Preece in 2007, this flash-based game is considered one of the pioneers of the tower defense genre. Players must build towers to stop waves of creeps from reaching the exit. Its simple yet addictive gameplay has made it a classic. Plants vs. Zombies: Developed by PopCap Games and released in 2009, this game adds a unique twist to the genre by featuring a humorous and charming aesthetic. If Plants vs Zombies was an industrial revolution in the history of tower defense games, I consider the emergence of Tomorrow's Ark to be a second industrial revolution.
